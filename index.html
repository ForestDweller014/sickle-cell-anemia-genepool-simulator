Sickle Cell Anemia Gene Pool Simulator

Each generation will have a specified number of offspring, and a mosquito with a specified chance of carrying malaria bites them. Sickle cell carriers will survive malaria, normal offspring will die from malaria and sickle cell inflicted offspring will die from the sickle cell disease. This will repeat until the gene pool is empty, if the bean lab simulation is used. In the realistic simulation, offspring will be made a specified number of times per generation. The allele frequencies are calculated.
